What it does

Hinglish & Tanglish

Hindi & Tamil subtitles rewritten in Roman letters (Hinglish / Tanglish), live and perfectly timed — so you read the words as they're spoken.

Offline meaning

An optional line shows the meaning in your language, translated on your device with Chrome's built-in AI. Nothing leaves your computer.

35+ languages

Translate into Tamil, English, Spanish, French, Japanese and many more — great for learning Hindi or Tamil from anywhere.

Private by design

No account, no tracking, no servers. Your settings stay on your device.

How it works

  1. Play a Hindi or Tamil title and turn on its original-language subtitles.
  2. Lipify rewrites them into Roman script on screen.
  3. (Optional) Pick your language, download the small on-device model once — a meaning line appears, fully offline.
