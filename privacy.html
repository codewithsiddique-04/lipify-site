Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Lipify ("the extension") is designed to protect your privacy. This policy explains what it does and does not do with your information.

Data we collect

None. Lipify does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

How it works

  • Subtitle romanization happens entirely in your browser. The extension reads the subtitle text already shown on the page and rewrites it in Latin script.
  • Meaning translation (optional) is performed on your device using Chrome's built-in translation model. The text being translated never leaves your computer and is not sent to any server.
  • Settings (on/off, display mode, subtitle size, your chosen language) are saved locally using the browser's storage and are not shared.

Network usage

Lipify makes no network requests to any server operated by us or any third party. The on-device translation model is downloaded and managed by Chrome itself, not by the extension.

Permissions

  • storage — to remember your settings on your device.
  • Host access to https://www.netflix.com — so the extension can read and rewrite the on-screen subtitles. It runs only on this site.

Third parties

Lipify is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Netflix or any other service. It does not share data with anyone.
